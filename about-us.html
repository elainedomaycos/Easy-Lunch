How It All Began

A journey of passion, flavor, and community

2020 Founded

Easy Lunch started with a simple dream in a small kitchen in Mabini, Batangas. What began as a passion project between friends has grown into a beloved local favorite, serving thousands of satisfied customers every month.

Our founder, EL, always believed that great food doesn't have to be complicated. After years of perfecting recipes and experimenting with flavors, the first batch of our signature buffalo wings was served to friends and family. The response was overwhelming.

Today, we're proud to be Mabini's go-to spot for comfort food that brings people together. Every wing, burger, and pizza is made with the same care and passion that started it all‚Äîreal ingredients, real teamwork, and hearts full of love for what we do.
